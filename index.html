About This Platform

The Autism Support Platform is designed to provide resources, tools, and support for individuals on the autism spectrum and those seeking to understand autism better.

Our mission is to:

  • Provide accessible screening tools for autism awareness
  • Offer speech therapy resources to improve communication
  • Enable sign language practice with AI assistance
  • Help users understand and recognize emotions

Disclaimer: This platform is for educational and support purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional for proper evaluation and diagnosis.
